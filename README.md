**Unleash your inner rhythm in $Neon$ $Pulse$, the cutting-edge, web-based rhythm game where your body is the controller.**

## Key Features

- Body-Powered Gameplay: Utilize real-time hand-tracking via your webcam.
- Vibrant Cyber-Minimalism: A pure, striking aesthetic of black backgrounds and high-saturation neon lines.
- Web-Optimized Performance: Runs seamlessly in your browser (Desktop Chrome recommended) with low latency.
- Rhythmic Flow: Engage in fast-paced, 128 BPM action requiring coordination and movement.
